Key Features

6-Wheeled Mobility

Capable of traversing tough terrains with continuous ground contact, ensuring stable navigation.

Sensor Array

Equipped with 4 distance sensors and light sensors to collect real-time data from the environment.

Easily Programmable

Easy to program in C/C++ and MicroPython.

How It Works

This robot uses its sensor array to navigate, collect data, and transmit it to a connected computer for processing.

It's designed to be a flexible platform for testing reinforcement learning algorithms and autonomous decision-making processes.

Applications

This robot can be used in a variety of environments, from research labs to industrial or hazardous locations, providing valuable data collection and testing opportunities for autonomous systems.
